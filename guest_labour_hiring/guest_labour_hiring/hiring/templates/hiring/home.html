About the System

The Guest Labour Hiring System is designed to streamline the process of managing migrant workers. It connects workers, employers, and police under a single platform with full transparency and accountability.

Developed as an IGNOU BCA Final Year Project, the system ensures secure, role-based access for all stakeholders.

Key Features

Police Verification

Workers are verified and issued NOCs by police authorities.

Job Postings

Employers post vacancies and manage applications easily.

Worker Management

Admin manages all worker registrations and profiles centrally.

Role-Based Access

4 separate roles: Admin, Police, Employer, and Worker.
